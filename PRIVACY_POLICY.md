# Privacy Policy — GizaScan

**Last updated:** April 2026

GizaScan is a free, open-source standalone educational application.

## We collect ZERO data

- No personal information collected, stored, or transmitted
- No analytics, tracking, cookies, or local storage
- No account creation, login, or authentication
- No location, camera, microphone, or contacts accessed
- No advertising or ad tracking

## Network Usage

The app loads Three.js from cdnjs.cloudflare.com and Google Fonts on first launch. After initial load, the app works fully offline. **No data is sent FROM your device.**

## Languages

The app auto-detects your browser language and displays in English, Portuguese (BR), Bulgarian, or German. Language preference is stored only in memory during the session.
